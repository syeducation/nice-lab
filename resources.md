# Resources
-------------------------

A large variety of resources can be found on Dr. Syed's [OSF Page](https://osf.io/profile/), 
in particular you will find:

[Teaching Materials](https://osf.io/avnqr/) - All teaching materials are 
[licensed cc0](https://creativecommons.org/public-domain/cc0/), 
meaning that anyone is free to use and alter them without permission.

[Mixed Methods Resources](https://osf.io/cs4h9/) - A series of video lectures on 
mixed methods that can be used for teaching. They can accompany the following open 
access article:

Syed, M., & Westberg, D. W. (2025). Mixed methods research in psychology: Rationales and research designs. *Advances in Methods and Practices in Psychological Science, 8*(2), 1–16. https://doi.org/10.1177/25152459251343919

[Coding Manuals for Narrative Data](https://osf.io/76eh5/)
